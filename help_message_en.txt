You can just send the expression you want to calculate to me,
or use command <code>/c ...</code> (or <code>/calc ...</code>, <code>/calculate ...</code>)

<b>Help</b>:
    Available operations: <code>+</code> (plus), <code>-</code> (minus), <code>*</code> (multi), <code>/</code> (divide)
    Use parentheses <code>(</code> and <code>)</code> to increase priority
    Multiplication and division have higher priority than addition
    <code>0</code> before decimal separator can be omitted

<b>Example</b>:
    <code>.1 + .1 + .1</code>
    <code>(20 + 1) * 2</code>
    <code>/c 114 / 514</code>
